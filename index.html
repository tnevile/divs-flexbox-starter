What Would John Hughes Do? is the latest performance work by Melbourne-based writer and performer, Telia Nevile. Utilising punk, poetry and intensely personal storytelling, it takes teen movies and TV as a starting point in an exploration of adulthood. Because adulthood isn't some magical destination that everyone else has arrived at and you still haven't found. We're all looking, all the time, and it's time we admitted it.
